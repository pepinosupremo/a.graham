Addison Graham is a fine artist from Fort Smith, Arkansas. She received her BFA from Arkansas Tech University in 2020 and is currently working on her MFA at the University of South Carolina.

Addisonâ€™s studio practice is inspired by natural and manmade landscapes, human emotion, and the various emotions that occur within specific locations. These inquiries and experiences are shown through expressive landscapes, mysterious figures, and surreal aspects interwoven within the composition. Addison continues to experiment with her emotions and relationship to a place using various forms of mixed media.

Graham has exhibited in multiple cities in Arkansas and was fortunate to be chosen as the first student solo exhibition at the Fort Smith Regional Art Museum in 2019.

She resides in Columbia, South Carolina to further develop her work as a graduate student and works as a graduate instructional assistant for introduction painting classes.
